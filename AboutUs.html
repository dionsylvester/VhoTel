Welcome to VhoTel Jakarta Indonesia

VhoTel is an iconic legendary 5-hotel sitting on the heart of Jakarta, Indonesia. Our luxury hotel has a strong foundation based on years of experience since 1999, preserved its original style of the 90s vintage with refined design and period furniture magnificently give you relaxation.


Our 27-floor hotel is like a "shelter" from trouble of life that creates time and space to make you feel safe, ease, and enjoy every day. The waiting room, the lounge, the bakeshop, the reading room, the musical area, and every single spots offer comfortness where guests can working privately, sipping first-class tea, or lazing on sun kissed pools over the metropolitan city views.


Designed in mind of natural environment and technology industry era. Embrace the destination through exploring major sightseeing places like the National Monument, also classy shopping scenes such as Grand Indonesia and Sudirman-Thamrin districts. Whether seeking the unforgettable journey or a memorable holiday.
